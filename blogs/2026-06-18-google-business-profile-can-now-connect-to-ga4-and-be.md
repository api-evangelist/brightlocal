Google announced two updates for Google Business Profile: integration with Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and a connection to Gemini AI. The GA4 connection lets businesses track GBP interactions, calls, bookings, and direction requests in one analytics dashboard, while the Gemini integration enables AI-assisted profile management through conversational commands, including review responses, real-time updates, and Business notebooks for organizing tasks and market recommendations.
